Depression Timeline

  • Day 1: Feeling unusually tired and unmotivated.
  • Day 2: Loss of interest in activities once enjoyed.
  • Day 3: Difficulty concentrating and making decisions.
  • Day 4: Changes in appetite and sleep patterns.
  • Day 5: Persistent feelings of sadness and hopelessness.
  • Day 6: Withdrawal from social interactions.
  • Day 7: Thoughts of self-harm or suicide (seek help immediately).
  • Day 8: Suicide or self harm may occur.(please reach help)
